Introduction to Fortnite

Fortnite is a popular online battle royale game developed by Epic Games, where players compete to be the last one standing on a vibrant, ever-changing island. It combines fast-paced action, creative building mechanics, and regular updates featuring new events, weapons, and collaborations with popular franchises.

Did you know The Fortnite World Cup has a prize pool of $30 million, so you can become a millionare just by playing Fortnite!
