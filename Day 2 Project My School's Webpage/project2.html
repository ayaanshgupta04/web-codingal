Bharti Vidyapeeth Rabindranath Tagore School of Excellence


Bharti Vidyapeeth Rabindranath Tagore School of Excellence is one of the best IGCSE schools in Pune.

BVRTSE is a IGCSE school in Pune, Maharashtra. It is an excellent good school.


About BVRTSE

BVRTSE was established in 2017, under the aegis of BHARTI VIDYAPEETH, a 50 year old educational conglomerate. BVRTSE is a Cambrige International School and is inspired by the teachings and philosophy of the Nobel laureate Shri Rabindranath Tagore.

BVRTSE offers the best Cambridge Pathway for students from Playschool to Grade 12.


Location

Located in Balewadi, Pune, BVRTSE allows ease of access from different parts of the city.

The school is located on a 3 acre site and is equipped with an environmental-friendly modern architecture with a world-class academic and sporting infrastructure.
