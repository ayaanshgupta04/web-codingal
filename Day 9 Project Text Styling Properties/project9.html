Typography with Food

Food, substance containing essentailly of protien, carbohydrate, fat and other nutrients, used in the body of an organism to sustain growth,conduct vital processes, and to furnish energy.
The absorbtion and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is facilitated by digestion.
Plants, which convert solor energy to food by photosynthesis, are the primary food source.

Animals that feed on plants often serve as sources of food for other animals.

Food is treated in a number of articles. For a description of the processes of absorbtion and utillization of food, see nutrition, human digestion, and the digestive system
