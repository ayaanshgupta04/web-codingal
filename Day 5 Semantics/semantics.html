Facts about dogs

Dogs have a sense of time. It has been proven that they know the difference between an hour and 5. Ifconditioned to, they can predcict the future events, such as regular walk times.

A study was conducted on dogs being away from their owners for varying hours and the studies show that the dogs that were away from their owners for the longest showed the greatest amount of affection.
